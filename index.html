🐦 Privacy Policy for Sky Puff

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Sky Puff ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Sky Puff is designed with privacy in mind:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any contact information
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies

Local Data Storage

The app stores your high scores locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not shared with any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

Sky Puff does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Advertising

Sky Puff contains no advertisements. We do not use any advertising networks or display any ads within the app.

In-App Purchases

Sky Puff does not contain any in-app purchases or monetization features.

Children's Privacy

Sky Puff is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The app complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Permissions

Sky Puff requests the following Android permissions:

  • VIBRATE - Used for haptic feedback when flapping and scoring

No other permissions are required.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches affecting your personal information through our app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. You can clear your local high scores at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > Sky Puff > Clear Data.
